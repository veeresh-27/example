HAMPI

Hampi or Hampe, also referred to as the Group of Monuments at Hampi, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site located in Hampi town, Vijayanagara district, east-central Karnataka, India.

Hampi was the capital of the Vijayanagara Empire in the 14th century!...

Location:

Hampi is situated on the banks of the Tungabhadra River in the eastern part of central Karnataka near the state border with Andhra Pradesh.

Special places in Hampi to Visit:

  • Virupaksha Temple
  • Vijaya Vittala Temple
  • Lotus Mahal
  • Yantrodharaka Hanuman Temple
  • Queen's Bath
  • Matanga Hill
  • Hemakuta Hill Temples
  • Archaeological Museum
